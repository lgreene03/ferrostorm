DIRECTORATE COMMAND - PRIORITY FLASH

They are coming through the Spine, Commander. Three columns, mixed armour
and those phantoms the survey teams keep reporting and the analysts keep
not believing in. Your gap is the only pass for forty kilometres.

You have two turrets, a thin garrison, and a refinery working the last
good field this side of the wall. There is no relief column. There is no
clever plan. There is the gap, and there is you in it.

Hold until the storm passes. That is the whole order. Hold.
