DIRECTORATE COMMAND - EYES ONLY

Commander. Eleven days ago the Ashvale extraction district went dark. The
official line is equipment failure. The truth is parked behind that ridge:
a raider camp flying no flag we recognise, bleeding our ferrite into
trucks that drive east and never come back.

You will take a construction detail and a small escort, stand up a
forward base, and remove the camp. Reinforcement credits are authorised
and will reach you by wire once your uplink stabilises.

One more thing. The raiders knew our patrol schedule. Nobody outside this
room had it. Build carefully, Commander - and trust the walls you pour
yourself.
