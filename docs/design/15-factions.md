# Factions of Ferrostorm

## Design law (learned the hard way, 2026-07-11)

The counter-triangle is COMMON. Rifle squads and rocket squads belong to
everyone; faction identity lives in the specials. We proved the alternative
empirically: locking rockets to one side handed it the hard counter to the
other side's line unit, and no amount of stat tuning could compensate (six
consecutive 6-0 sweeps in the faction gate).

## The Ferric Directorate ("the wall")

The superpower. Their telling: ferrite is civilisation's last structural
metal, and only central stewardship prevents a scramble that burns the
world. Lawful, industrial, paternal - and authoritarian to the bone.
Doctrine: expensive, durable, direct. You see the Directorate coming and
that is the point.

Specials: cannon tank (line armour), bulwark tank (the wall that walks,
crushes screens), howitzer (siege splash), sentinel scout (detection),
Skyhammer superweapon doctrine, escort doctrine (sentinels shadow
harvesters).

## The Sodality of Rust ("the shadow")

The insurgency. Their telling: the Directorate stole the fields from the
people who mined them, and everything since is repossession. Charismatic,
righteous, and increasingly willing to do terrible things.
Doctrine: cheap, fast, unseen. The Directorate wins the battles it can
find; the Sodality makes sure it finds very few.

Specials: shade raider (stealth harasser), phantom tank (rockets from
nowhere), veil projector (area cloak, brown-out fragile), shadow-war
doctrine (phantoms strangle enemy harvesting rather than join the line).

## The Reclaimers ("the tide") - lore now, mechanics later

Scavenger-guilds of the cinder margins. Their telling: both great powers are
already dead and simply have not stopped moving; the Reclaimers are the
burial detail, and salvage is the only honest inheritance.
Signature when they arrive: an economy of theft - capture, salvage,
conversion of wrecks. The engineer mechanic (common today) becomes their
doctrine writ large. Campaign missions should seed them from mission three
onward: unexplained salvage crews, stripped battlefields, a third voice on
the wire.

## Faction balance status (honest)

The 4x4 unit-counter gate is BLOCKING and green. The faction-war gate
(6 seeded AI matches) is REPORTING ONLY: eleven forge iterations oscillated
between shutouts because AI-vs-AI outcomes are doctrine-dominated - each
doctrine addition exposed the opponent AI's blind spot, ending with a
faithful reproduction of the classic 1995 harass-bait exploit (fast cloaked
units puppeting the defence trigger until the army died piecemeal, army
annihilated 25-0 between t=2800 and t=4200). Findings banked along the way:
the sentinel tax, value-per-factory-hour vs per-credit costing, the opening
tempo hole (nobody opens with their Mammoth), the common-triangle law
above, and the puppet exploit. Promotion to blocking is gated on
TICKET-AI-05 (defence squads: a dedicated guard force so the main army
ignores bait) and human playtesting once the Godot client runs.
