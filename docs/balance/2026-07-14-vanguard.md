## Journal: TICKET-P4-SLICE-01, the vanguard car forge

Design intent: the Directorate harasser - the shade raider trade taken the
other way, armour instead of stealth. Must shred infantry per-cost, must
lose to anything with an anti-armour gun.

Iteration log, one dial at a time:
1. First cut (450cr, 220hp Light, rifle gun, speed 0.32): HARD FAIL - a
   rifle-gun carrier at 2.25x rifle cost loses the per-cost dps race to
   massed rifles by arithmetic. The gate said so on the first run.
2. New weapon (VanguardGun: range 4, 18 dmg AntiInfantry, cooldown 6):
   fixed rifles, inverted the ROCKET counter - the autocannon melted 80hp
   launchers before their 20-tick cycle landed twice.
3. Range dial 4 to 3: exposed a HARNESS pathology, not a unit one - the
   first partial-wipe standoff in the gate's history. Plain Attack orders
   end when their target dies; one surviving car and four launchers sat
   passive out of sight range for 2700 ticks. Diagnosed by direct
   reproduction (1v1 resolves in 43 ticks; 6v10 froze at t=300 with
   fixed positions). Fix in the harness: armies now AttackMove to the
   enemy muster point and hunt to the finish - which is also the more
   honest model of a fight. All previously committed matchup verdicts
   held under the new harness (triangle, siege knife-fight law, specials
   losing fair fights); survivor percentages shifted, as expected.
4. Hp dial 220 to 150: rockets' AntiArmour volleys became decisive.
   PASS - vanguard > rifle 60% sv, rocket > vanguard 40% sv,
   cannon > vanguard 100% sv, and the car still bullies siege up close.

Also recorded: the howitzer mirror is UNRESOLVED under attack-move (two
dead-zone armies walking into mutual minimum range and declining to fire).
Mirrors are unconstrained by design; noted for the siege rework ticket.
Goldens byte-identical throughout - the catalogue addition is invisible
until a match actually spawns type 12.
